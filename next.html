Ma'am Loida's advice and tips for upcoming STI'ers

First of all, study hard because we are here at school to study and to learn. Second,
just enjoy your college life. Because once you finish college and start working. You will miss your college life, as long as there are times when the things
the professors ask you to do are very challenging. Just enjoy it because that's part of being a student.That's where you learn, that's where you start.
So if you gave up here at school, what's more in reality the days you worked. It seems that school is just like your stepping stone, but when you are there
in the real world. It seems like the real thing is there, so we should improve it now. You should learn patience especially if you are already working on a long thesis and then always obey your parents and then choose a passion that will take you up not down.
