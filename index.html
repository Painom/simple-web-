Cruz

Age: 42
Birth: February 5,1980
Address: Bunsuran 3rd Pandi,Bulacan

"Ms. Loida Cruz" is one of the first pioneer that graduated in STI Meycauayan.
She's also the school admimistrator of STI Meycauayan. She started at STI
wayback "1997" with a course of BSCS (Bachelor of Science in Computer Science)
She choosed STI because back in 1997 computer courses is very indemand. She faced so many struggles because they don't always
use computers. One of Ma'am Loida's struggle is the place, since Ma'am lived in Pandi Bulacan, she used to commute at exactly 4am
just to make it on time. Ma'am Loida's struggle is also the mircrosoft softwares since they are not that familiar with these particular apps.
Ma'am Loida said "It's part of your college life" without struggles your college life is not colorful.

Ma'am Loida overcome these challenges by focusing on her studies. She want to finish her studies because it is her stepping stone for a better future.
She also said that praying is important, she prayed for her better future and to make herself strong because there is always the time that you'll feel
like giving up. Her perspective that someday she can help her family helped her so overcome these struggles.
